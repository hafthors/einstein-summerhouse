Restaurant GREEN TREE

Come and relax in the nature

Our Restaurant serves Asian Food and Fusion of Japanese and Thai.We are direct by the lake of Griebnitzsee where you can have a nice walk along the lake as well to go for kayak tour. You can enjoy the beautiful sunsets from the Restaurant while you eat or afterwards by the lake.

We are famous for our Fusion CuisineFusion cuisine includes sushi burritos (Japanese and Mexican), ramen burgers (Japanese and American), Korean tacos (Korean and Mexican), butter chicken pizza (Indian and Italian), and Tex-Mex dishes like fajitas and quesadillas.
